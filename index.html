About

Hello, world: I'm Lucrezia!

I am a molecular biologist interested in Stem Cell & Developmental Biology. Over the course of the past 4 years I have been working in several renowned intitutes around Europe, where I had the chance to dive deeper into the fascinating world of cell fate determination and morphogenesis. I was involved in the setting up of several projects and trained to work with multiple cell types and model organisms. During this time, I started my journey into programming and image analysis, which are now one of my main passions.

At the moment, I am based in Lisbon, where I am working as a PhD candidate in the laboratory of Dr. Caren Norden. My current project focuses on the mechanisms that coordinate proliferation and differentiation in the retinal neuroepithelium and involves the use of cutting-edge microscopy techniques such as AiryScan microscopy, Lightsheet Fluorescence Microscopy and deep learning tools for image processing.
